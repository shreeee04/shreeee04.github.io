Smart Health & Driving Safety System (SHDSS)

AI, IoT, and ML-powered system for real-time driving safety

Abstract

Road accidents, often caused by fatigue, drunk driving, and poor health, require proactive safety measures. The Smart Health & Driving Safety System (SHDSS) enhances road safety through real-time health monitoring, fatigue detection, and intelligent vehicle control. Key features include Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB), drowsiness detection, alcohol monitoring, and adaptive speed control for adverse conditions.

The system slows down the vehicle for low health or medium alcohol levels and prevents startup for high alcohol levels, prioritizing drunk driving prevention. It also uses eye tracking to confirm if the driver is intoxicated, utilizing machine learning for verification and integrating the system into the seat belt, ensuring it focuses only on the driver.
